Good Kisser (2019)

Description:

Jenna and Kate, a couple in a two-year rut, decide to spice things up with a ménage à trois. Enter Mia, a mesmerizing stranger who sets their bedroom ablaze with passion. But as the night unfolds, the thrill of forbidden desire unravels their relationship's fragile foundation. Jealousy, insecurities, and hidden secrets emerge as the lines blur between playfulness and possessiveness. Jenna grapples with her attraction to Mia, while Kate questions their entire dynamic. Can their love triangle survive the heat, or will it ignite a devastating explosion of betrayal and heartbreak? "Good Kisser" is a steamy and suspenseful exploration of intimacy, vulnerability, and the complexities of modern love. It's a rollercoaster of emotions, packed with twists, turns, and enough erotic tension to make your heart race.

    **Here are some additional details you might want to know:**

  • * The film is directed by Wendy Jo Carlton and features strong female leads.
  • * It delves into themes of female sexuality, bisexuality, and open relationships.
  • * Be prepared for explicit content and mature themes.
If you're looking for a thought-provoking and provocative drama that challenges traditional relationship dynamics, "Good Kisser" is a must-watch. Just remember to keep the fan handy!
