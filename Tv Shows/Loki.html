Loki (2023)

Season 1
Season 2

Description:

Loki Season 1 (2021): After accidentally creating a new timeline during the events of Avengers: Endgame, a mischievous Loki is apprehended by the Time Variance Authority (TVA), a mysterious organization responsible for maintaining the correct flow of time. Offered a choice: be erased from existence or help fix the timeline he disrupted, Loki reluctantly becomes a TVA agent, traveling through time with Mobius M. Mobius to hunt down a variant of himself causing chaos. As their mission progresses, Loki confronts his identity, purpose, and the complexities of love. He uncovers shocking truths about the TVA and its creators, leading to a climactic battle that shatters the timeline and opens the door to a multiverse. Loki Season 2 (2023): Following the destruction of the Sacred Timeline in season 1, Loki finds himself in a strange new TVA, one ruled by different variants of Renslayer. He reunites with Mobius and Hunter B-15, and together they navigate the branching timelines, searching for Sylvie, the variant Loki who escaped with the TVA's founder, He Who Remains. Their journey takes them to various historical and fantastical settings, as they unravel the mysteries surrounding He Who Remains and the true purpose of the TVA. Loki grapples with his responsibility for the multiverse's chaos and faces difficult choices about love, forgiveness, and his own potential for growth.
