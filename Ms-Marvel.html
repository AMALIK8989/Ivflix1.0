Ms Marvel (2022)

Description:

Ms. Marvel" follows Kamala Khan, a teen Avenger fan who gains cosmic powers from a mystical bangle. As she grapples with controlling her abilities, she uncovers family secrets and clashes with the Clandestines, seeking to breach a dimensional barrier. Kamala navigates alliances, confronts her heritage, and ultimately embraces her destiny as "Ms. Marvel," becoming a symbol of hope while facing off against the DODC and discovering unexpected connections to Captain Marvel and the bangle's power.
