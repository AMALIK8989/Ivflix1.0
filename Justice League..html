Justice League (2021)

Description:

"In a distant past, the Apokoliptian warlord Darkseid and his Parademons failed to conquer Earth with the help of the powerful Mother Boxes, thwarted by a united alliance of gods, Amazons, Atlanteans, humans, and extraterrestrial beings. These Mother Boxes were scattered and hidden across the world. Fast forward to the present, the death of Superman triggers the reactivation of the Mother Boxes, drawing the attention of Steppenwolf, Darkseid's disgraced lieutenant. Steppenwolf seeks to regain his master's favor by gathering the boxes and using them to reshape Earth into a replica of their homeworld, Apokolips. Steppenwolf's quest leads him to Themyscira, where he battles the Amazons to obtain one of the Mother Boxes. This event prompts Diana Prince and Bruce Wayne to come together and form a team of metahumans to defend the planet. Despite initial recruitment setbacks, they eventually enlist the aquatic hero Arthur Curry, the speedster Barry Allen, and the cybernetic Victor Stone, whose father is kidnapped by Parademons searching for their hidden Mother Box. Victor reveals that the Mother Box was used to rebuild his body after a car accident, explaining the technology's matter-altering capabilities. Realizing the potential to resurrect Superman, the team unearths Clark Kent's body and utilizes the Genesis Chamber in a Kryptonian ship. However, a temporarily amnesiac Superman perceives them as a threat upon his resurrection. Lois Lane plays a crucial role in calming him and helping him regain his memories. Meanwhile, Steppenwolf has a vision of the Anti-Life Equation on Earth, a power sought by Darkseid. The team races to stop Steppenwolf from forming the Unity, a catastrophic event that would tear the planet apart. In the abandoned Russian city of Pozharnov, they engage in a battle against Parademons and ultimately receive timely assistance from a revived Superman. With Victor and Superman's combined efforts, they manage to thwart the Unity, defeating Steppenwolf. Darkseid, however, vows to return to Earth in pursuit of the elusive Anti-Life Equation. In the epilogue, plans are made to establish a headquarters for the superhero team at Wayne Manor. The heroes return to their everyday lives: Clark resumes his duties in Metropolis, Arthur meets with allies in Atlantis, and Barry secures a job in Central City's police department. Victor finds purpose in a message left by his late father. Meanwhile, Lex Luthor escapes from Arkham Asylum and reveals Batman's secret identity to Slade Wilson. In a pre-credit scene, Bruce Wayne has a foreboding dream about an apocalyptic future and is visited by the Martian Manhunter, who offers his assistance in preparing for Darkseid's inevitable return.
