Captain America The Winter Soldier (2014)

Description:

Captain America, now adjusted to the 21st century, teams up with Black Widow and Falcon to uncover a conspiracy within S.H.I.E.L.D., the very organization he once trusted. He faces a mysterious assassin known as the Winter Soldier and grapples with the shocking truth about his past. The film delves into political intrigue, technological advancements, and the blurred lines between loyalty and freedom.
